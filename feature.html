RISE is a fashion forward retailer that blends local culture with global trends creating a unique spin on fashion. Pioneering Streetwear into the Bangladesh market, RISE offers a wide array of clothing and accessories.

Rise puts emphasis on style and fashion. Streetwear influenced, the brand produces a wide range of clothing from premium denim to ethnic wear. Rise infuses street wear with cultural elements adding a unique new element to its styling. Apart from clothing, the brand offers a wide range of accessories that will complete any look.
